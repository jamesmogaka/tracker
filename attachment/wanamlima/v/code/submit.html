Upon submission, you'll receive a welcoming message: "Welcome to our website! We're excited to have you join our community. One of the key features of our platform is the ability to access and manage your personal account, where you have the freedom to customize preferences, settings, and view your data. To get started with your personalized experience, simply identify yourself as a registered user by clicking on the ‘Login’ button, conveniently located in the top right corner of the homepage. Once logged in, you'll have the power to tailor your experience to your liking, exploring the various features and engaging with the vibrant community of Friends of Ngong Hills. Customize your preferences, stay updated on events, and participate in discussions that align with your interests. It's important to note that when you've completed your tasks and are ready to leave the site, we prioritize the security of your account. Ensure a secure and private browsing experience by logging out. You can do this easily by locating the ‘Logout’ button, which is often situated in the same area as the 'Login' button. Clicking ‘Logout’ will disconnect you from your account, providing an added layer of security. Feel free to explore the wealth of features and resources our platform offers. If you have any questions or need assistance along the way, our support team is here to help. We hope you enjoy your time on Friends of Ngong Hills, where community, engagement, and security are our top priorities. Welcome aboard!"
